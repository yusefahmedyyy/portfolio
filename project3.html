PRODUCT DETAIL SCREEN

The Product Detail Screen provides detailed information about a selected product. It includes a carousel of product images, product title, brand, price, description, and options for selecting color and size. Users can adjust the quantity and use buttons to add the product to their wishlist or proceed to the order page.

HOME SCREEN

The Home Screen is the main entry point of the app. It features a navigation drawer with user profile information and menu items. The main area displays a grid of products, each with an image, title, rating, and price. Tapping a product navigates to the Product Detail Screen.

PRODUCT ITEM WIDGET

The Product Item widget represents an individual product within the grid. It displays a product image, title, rating, and price, and handles navigation to the Product Detail Screen when tapped.
